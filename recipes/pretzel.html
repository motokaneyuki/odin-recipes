Pretzel

Description

Pretzel Hershey kiss treats topped with M&Ms candies. These are really fun to make with the kids for a wonderful chocolate treat. Recipe by LCDOYLE from allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) package small pretzel twists
  • 1 (8 ounce) package milk chocolate candy kisses (such as Hershey's Kisses), unwrapped
  • 1 (1.69 ounce) package candy-coated milk chocolate pieces (such as M&M's)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees F (80 degrees C).
  2. Arrange pretzels on a baking sheet in a single layer. Place a candy kiss on the center of each pretzel.
  3. Warm pretzels in the preheated oven until candy kisses are shiny and slightly softened, about 2 minutes.
  4. Place a candy-coated chocolate piece atop the candy kiss on each pretzel; press down. Chill in the refrigerator for 10 minutes.
